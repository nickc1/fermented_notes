# Pizza Crust

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup wheat flour
- 1 cup bread flour
- 1/2 cup sourdough starter
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp Olive Oil
- Water to combine

## Directions
- Combine all ingredients to form a shaggy ball.
- Let ferment for at least 24 hours
- Press out into a well oiled cast iron pan
- Bake on 375 for 30 minutes or until it is crispy
